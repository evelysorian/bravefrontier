Company

The game was first announced after the Gaming Company A-Lim is officially registered in March 2013. The company, formed from the combined efforts of Gumi Ventures, B-Dash Ventures, and Fuji Startup Ventures, a company owned by Fuji TV.

Gumi's slogan is One Step Beyond. An intersting fact about them is that they own the volcaloids which are cumputerized voices that are used for singing. Since they had the rights for the volcaliods they put the volcaliods in the game without any problems.

Gumi also helps make other games like Chain Chronicle, Big Hero 6 Bot Fight, Puzzule Trooper, and Slots Reel Frontier. All these games are avaliable at the Apple Store and at Google play and some of them are avabliable at Amazon apps. They specialize in making games on IOS and Andriod.

Gumi's company is located in the Southeast Asian countries of Singapore, the Philippines and Indonesia.
