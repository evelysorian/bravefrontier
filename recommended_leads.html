Leader Skills

In Brave Frontier every 3 star and above has a leader skill. If you make a unit of your choice the leader of your squad then their leader skill will active. There are many great units out thier with amazing leader skills. The only problem is that different leader skills are needed for different batles. I'll talk about recommended leader skill and squads for any type of challenge.

Quest: for regular questing any leader skill of a 5 star and above should be goon since in question you should rely more on the strength of your units than your leader skill. One warning I do say say is don't be reckless. Be sure to have a healer even if it's stats aren't very good and thats basically it for regular questing.

Arena: for arena you should have a team fully based on attack since all you do in arena is fight against other players. Any unit that has a leader skill of bosting attack is good for a leader for your arena team.

Raid: in raid you go against bosses so much stronger than previous bosses in regular questing. Since the bosses are much more powerful you should have a team mostly based on attack, defense, and the team should have very high health. So a unit with a leaderskill that boots attack, defense, and health will help you survive. One last thing, I highly recommend a mitigator on your team. Mitigators are also known as units that reduce the dammage taken.

Grand Quest: the tips for raid is the same for grand quest but I do recommend a healer and make sure you main team is the strongest since most quests in grand quest let you have more than one team. Note: for any different challenges tyour team will be more reliable if it was a team full of 7 stars. Any of the leaderskills bellow you be good for any challenge.
